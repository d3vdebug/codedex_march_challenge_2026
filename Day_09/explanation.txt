To solve the problem of counting unique words in a transcript, I first convert the entire text to lowercase so that words like “Hello” and “hello” are treated as the same word. Then I use a regular expression to remove all punctuation by deleting any character that isn’t a letter, a number, or a space. This leaves behind only clean words. After that, I split the text into individual words and filter out any empty entries that might appear. Finally, I convert the list of words into a set, which automatically removes duplicates, and return the size of that set.
