# Codédex March Challenge 2026

Welcome to the **Codédex March Challenge 2026**! This is a fun and exciting daily coding adventure where participants tackle one programming puzzle each day for the entire month of March. Whether you're a beginner looking to build skills or an experienced coder seeking a creative outlet, these challenges offer something for everyone.

## 🌟 What is the March Challenge?

The Codédex March Challenge is a month-long event featuring 30 unique daily challenges. Each day brings a new theme inspired by holidays, historical events, pop culture, and more. From word games to space exploration, from mathematical puzzles to emoji history, there's always something interesting to explore.

The challenges are designed to be engaging and educational, helping you practice Python programming while learning about fascinating topics. You can solve them at your own pace, share your solutions with the community, and even compete on leaderboards.
