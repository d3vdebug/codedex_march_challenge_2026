I built an analyze( ) function to track a company’s progress in “closing the gap.” It returns three metrics: Net Change Per Year (NCPY), Trend, and Dips. 

NCPY is computed as (last − first) / (years − 1) and rounded to 4 decimals for clear reporting. The trend compares the first 3‑year average and the last 3‑year average; if the last is higher, it’s "improving," equal is "stagnating," and otherwise it's “declining.” 

Finally, Dips counts how many years decreased vs. the previous year.
