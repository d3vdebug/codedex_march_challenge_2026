My Wordle function works by comparing each letter in the guessed word directly with the corresponding letter in the secret word and counting how many of those positions match exactly. 

Since Wordle words are always five letters long, the loop runs five times, checking one character at each position. Every time the letter in guess[x] is the same as secret[x], the counter increases by one. 

After completing the loop, the function returns this count, which represents how many letters the player guessed correctly in the right spot, just like the green tiles in Wordle.
